## Hackathon Tracks & Prizes

### Track 1 — ElevenLabs: Autonomous Tenant & Vendor Operations

**Track prize:** 6 months of ElevenLabs Scale tier per team member ($1,980 value each)

**Challenge:** Tenant communication and service coordination are repetitive, manual, and time-consuming. Maintenance requests, vendor coordination, and status updates often require endless back-and-forth communication.

**Goal:** Design an AI-powered operations layer that automates communication, coordination, and service workflows across tenants, landlords, and vendors. Think AI-powered operations teams that are always on.

**Challenge prompts:**
- "What if 80% of tenant requests never needed a human?"
- "How do we coordinate vendors without back-and-forth chaos?"
- "What would a fully automated maintenance workflow look like?"

**Inspo:**
- Automated workflows for repairs, inquiries, and updates
- AI chat + voice interfaces
- Vendor/internal team task routing
- Status tracking and communication loops

**Bonus points:**
- Real-time voice AI call handling
- Multi-channel orchestration (email, SMS, phone)
- Sentiment detection and escalation logic
- End-to-end resolution tracking

**Outcome vision:** An always-on operations engine that handles communication and coordination with minimal human input.

---

### Track 2 — Stripe: Autonomous Payment Operations *(this is our track)*

**Track prize:** **$5,000 USD in Stripe fee credits** (Payments, Connect, Billing, Tax, and Stripe Sigma)

**Challenge:** Property management payments are fragmented, manual, and error-prone — spanning tenants, landlords, and vendors. Late payments, reconciliation issues, and admin overhead slow everything down. Reimagine how money moves between tenants, landlords, and vendors — with automation, intelligence, and zero manual overhead.

**Goal:** Build an automated, intelligent payment system that handles the full payment lifecycle:
- Rent collection from tenants
- Vendor payouts (maintenance, services)
- Owner/landlord disbursements
- Smart reminders, retries, and exception handling

Think "set-and-forget" finance infrastructure for property operations.

**Challenge prompts:**
- "What would a fully autonomous rent collection system look like?"
- "How do we eliminate payment-related support tickets entirely?"
- "Can payments adapt dynamically to tenant or vendor behavior?"

**Inspo:**
- Automated payment flows using Stripe
- Logic for scheduling, retries, and failed payments
- Smart notifications (late rent, anomalies)
- Simple dashboard or API layer for visibility
- Automated payments for vendors

**Bonus points** *(RentPilot AI hits all three)***:**
- AI-driven risk detection (late payer prediction, anomalies)
- Dynamic payment plans or negotiation flows
- Reconciliation automation across multiple parties

**Outcome vision:** A financial operations layer that makes payments invisible, reliable, and stress-free.

### Track 3 — AI Property Operations Copilot

**Track prize:** Overall winning prizes

**Challenge:** Property managers spend hours every day searching for information, answering repetitive questions, coordinating workflows, and switching between fragmented systems. Emails, tickets, contracts, invoices, meeting notes, and vendor conversations all contain critical operational context — but none of it is unified. AI assistants today still struggle with long-term memory, contextual reasoning, and reliable operational execution.

**Goal:** Build an AI-powered operations copilot that acts like a high-performing property operations teammate. The system should understand context across properties, retrieve the right information instantly, reason through operational tasks, and assist humans with decision-making and execution. Think Claude, but purpose-built for property operations.

**Challenge prompts:**
- "What would the ideal AI teammate for a property manager look like?"
- "How can AI reduce operational overwhelm without removing human oversight?"
- "What workflows should AI handle autonomously vs collaboratively?"
- "How do we make AI responses trustworthy and context-aware?"

**Inspo:**
- Conversational AI interface for operators/property managers
- Context-aware retrieval across emails, tickets, documents, and systems
- Workflow execution capabilities (summaries, drafting, task generation, follow-ups)
- Long-context memory and reasoning capabilities
- Human-in-the-loop approval flows for sensitive actions

**Bonus points:**
- Multi-step reasoning and task planning
- Persistent memory across conversations and properties
- AI-generated operational recommendations
- Natural language querying across structured + unstructured data
- Real-time collaboration between humans and AI agents

**Outcome vision:** An intelligent operations copilot that transforms property management from reactive administration into proactive, AI-assisted decision-making.

## Key takeaways for RentPilot AI

(Brief annotations from our team — not part of the verbatim brief.)

1. **The Stripe track is property-payment-specific.** Not generic Stripe usage. Our scope must stay in the tenant + landlord + vendor world.
2. **"Dynamic payment plans or negotiation flows"** is literally a bonus point. The original RentPlan AI direction was correctly targeted; what needed fixing was the depth (real judgment, multi-party, more Stripe surface).
3. **We hit all three Stripe-track bonus points** with the committed scope. See `docs/project.md`.
4. **Submission has two artifacts:** 2-min Loom + public GitHub. The repo's README is part of the judged surface — keep it tidy.
5. **5-minute live pitch** comes after submission. Demo flow in `docs/project.md` is built around this.
6. **Overall winner is selected across tracks.** ElevenLabs voice (stretch goal) could give us a shot at it without abandoning the Stripe track.
